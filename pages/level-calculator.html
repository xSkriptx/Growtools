About Level Calculation

This calculator uses the official Growtopia leveling formula to determine how much XP you need to reach your target level.

How It Works

The XP required for each level follows the formula: 50 × (level² + 2). The calculator sums up the XP required for all levels between your current level and target level, then subtracts your current XP.

Example

If you're at level 5 with 500 XP and want to reach level 10:

  • XP needed for level 6: 50 × (6² + 2) = 1,900 XP
  • XP needed for level 7: 50 × (7² + 2) = 2,550 XP
  • XP needed for level 8: 50 × (8² + 2) = 3,300 XP
  • XP needed for level 9: 50 × (9² + 2) = 4,150 XP
  • XP needed for level 10: 50 × (10² + 2) = 5,100 XP
  • Total XP needed: 1,900 + 2,550 + 3,300 + 4,150 + 5,100 = 17,000 XP
  • Minus your current XP: 17,000 - 500 = 16,500 XP needed
